// About

Development & consulting

Muneris is an independent software studio focused on hospitality and point-of-sale integration. The tools on this page came out of real client work — small, sharp utilities built to remove friction from day-to-day operations.

AI is baked into how Muneris works. Many tools ship with agent-friendly interfaces — clean CLIs and structured output — so they can be driven directly by Claude Code, Codex or similar agents, not just by hand.

Beyond the free tools, Muneris takes on consulting and custom development engagements: system integrations, automation, and bespoke internal tooling — primarily on the .NET stack.
